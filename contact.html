Frequently Asked Questions

How can I register for the workshop?

Registration for Gaze4NLP 2025 is handled through the main ACL 2025 conference registration system. Visit the ACL website and select the Gaze4NLP workshop during registration.

Is remote participation possible?

Yes, we will offer a hybrid format that allows for remote participation. Details about virtual access will be sent to registered participants closer to the workshop date.

Will the presentations be recorded?

Yes, all keynote presentations and paper sessions will be recorded and made available to registered participants after the workshop. Selected content will also be publicly available on our website.

How can I become a sponsor?

We offer various sponsorship packages for organizations interested in supporting the workshop. Please contact our sponsorship team at sponsors@gaze4nlp2025.org for more information.
